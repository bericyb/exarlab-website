# Fun
## Hiking
Since 2023 I've had some pretty bad knee pain when running or walking for a while. I owe this to sitting crosslegged and being sedentary for long periods of time. I think getting more active on my feet again, without a bike, would help this. 
On the 15th I hiked from Dry Canyon to the peak of Mt. Baldy. Big mistake. I figured that hiking would be a safe bet instead of running. Endurance wise, I was fine and made it to the top no problem. The issue was coming back down. Walking downhill applied much more pressure to my already tired knees and I was in incredible pain and ran out of water/food. With the help of a walking stick nearly as tall as me, and a nice slow pace I made it down after 3.5 hours of hiking.
More short trips are in order. I've been trying to train my knees with just more reps on the stairs in my home in the meantime to recover.
